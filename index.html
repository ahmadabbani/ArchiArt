Frequently Asked Questions

01
What printing materials do you offer for billboards?

At ArchiArt, we offer premium quality vinyl, mesh, and eco-friendly materials for billboard printing. Our vinyl options include high-durability UV-resistant materials perfect for outdoor advertisements, while our mesh options allow for better airflow in windy locations. We also provide recyclable options for environmentally conscious businesses.

02
What is the turnaround time for wall murals?

Our standard turnaround time for wall murals is 5-7 business days from final design approval to completion. For rush orders, we offer expedited services that can deliver in as little as 48 hours depending on project complexity and size. Custom installation timelines may vary based on location and mural dimensions.

03
How long do your vinyl prints typically last outdoors?

Our premium outdoor vinyl prints are designed to last 3-5 years with full color vibrancy when properly installed. This includes resistance to UV fading, water damage, and temperature fluctuations. For extended durability, we offer special lamination options that can increase lifespan to 7+ years even in challenging weather conditions.

04
Do you provide installation services for large format prints?

Yes, ArchiArt offers professional installation services for all large format prints including billboards, wall murals, and architectural wraps. Our certified installation team operates nationwide and ensures perfect application with attention to surface preparation, precision alignment, and bubble-free results. Installation services can be added to any printing order.

05
What file formats do you accept for printing projects?

We accept a wide range of file formats including AI, PDF, PSD, TIFF, JPG, and EPS. For optimal print quality, we recommend vector files (AI, PDF, EPS) for logos and graphics, and high-resolution (300+ DPI) raster files for photographic elements. Our design team can work with your existing files or help convert them to print-ready formats at no additional charge.
